Adopt and animal and listen to its heartbeat!

Help stop the extinction of animals. Your commitment makes a difference. You can "adopt" an animal at Anisave and give it better chances to live a good life in nature, as well as listen to its heartbeat though our app. We at Anisave hade developed a way to stream the heartbeat of a wild animal of your choosing directly to your phone via the use of satelites.

How does it work?

To save help save the wild animals, you create a user account on this page. Then, you choose the animal you want to "adopt" and Pay the donation. The money goes to improve the livelihood of the animal you have adopted. Once the transaction has been completed, you will be able to follow the animal in real time on your very own phone via our app, or directly on this page. This is only possible with Anisaves newly invented satellite technology. You will have the opportunity to follow where the animal is at all times with our interactive map. You will receive updates on what the money you have donated is used for, as well as how the condition is in the habitat where the animal lives. Our volunteer corps will then take pictures of the animal and send them to you if it is possible and we do not unnecessarily disturb the animals in any way. We have also used modern technology that can register the heartbeat of the animal you have adopted. This way you will feel close to the animal at all times and develop your very own bond to a wild animal.

Why is it important?

Earth's wildlife is disappearing at a furious pace. In a cynical world, where the animals' livelihoods are at the expense of economic profit, mass extinction of endangered species is imminent. Anisave is the animals' advocate. Together we can prevent the degradation of the natural areas where the animals live and facilitate the best possible living conditions for these animals. Because we believe that the animals of todays age should live on for their and our next generations sake, and with your help we can make that dream a reality.
